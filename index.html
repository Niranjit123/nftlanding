Q&A

We are a huge marketplace dedicated to connecting great artists of all Metalink with their fans and unique token collectors!

  • NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token. It's a digital asset that represents ownership or proof of authenticity of a unique item or piece of content, such as artwork, music, videos, or even virtual real estate, using blockchain technology.

  • Purchasing an NFT from our collection is easy! Simply browse our collection on the OpenSea platform, select the NFT you're interested in, and follow the prompts to complete your purchase using cryptocurrency.

  • Our NFT collection is built on the Ethereum blockchain, utilizing the ERC-721 token standard. This ensures transparency, security, and interoperability within the broader NFT ecosystem.

  • As a decentralized marketplace, there are generally no resale restrictions on the NFTs you purchase from our collection. Once you own an NFT, you have the freedom to sell, trade, or transfer it as you see fit.

  • Each NFT in our collection comes with a unique digital signature that is recorded on the blockchain, providing immutable proof of ownership and authenticity. You can verify the authenticity of your NFT by checking its smart contract address and transaction history on the Ethereum blockchain.
